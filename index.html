Our Story

Sepurukuri College was founded by Lucila Sepulcri, a visionary educator who believed in the power of technology to
transform education. Lucila envisioned a place where students could immerse themselves in interactive learning
environments, participate in virtual field trips, and collaborate on projects in ways that were not possible in the physical
world.
