Japan Virtual College

This name represents an online educational platform offering virtual courses and degree programs focusing on Japanese culture, language, and technology. It aims to attract international students interested in studying Japanese subjects from anywhere in the world.

Site Purpose

The site provides a platform for students to enroll in virtual courses, access educational resources, and interact with instructors. It also offers information on various degree programs, admission requirements, and student support services.

Courses

  • What are the available degree programs in Japanese language and culture?
  • How can I enroll in a virtual course on Japanese technology trends?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #1f4e79 (Dark Blue) - used for headers and navigation bar.

Secondary Color: #f7f7f7 (Light Gray) - used for background and content sections.

Accent Color: #e60012 (Red) - used for buttons and links.

Typography

Header Font: 'Noto Sans JP', sans-serif - used for all headings.

Body Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif - used for all paragraph text.
